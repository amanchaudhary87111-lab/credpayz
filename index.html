Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you transfer. Still unsure? Just WhatsApp us.

How fast does the money reach my account?+

Most transfers are completed instantly via IMPS/RTGS — typically within 5 to 15 minutes during working hours. For larger amounts, it may take slightly longer as we verify everything for your safety.

Will this show as a cash withdrawal on my card?+

No. The transaction processes as a standard POS merchant purchase — not a cash advance. This means you avoid the high cash-advance interest rates and fees that banks charge on ATM withdrawals.

Which cards and networks do you support?+

We support all major networks — Visa, MasterCard, and RuPay. Both personal and most card types are accepted. Just share your card details on WhatsApp and we'll confirm.

Is Aadhaar or PAN required?+

For standard transactions, we keep the process simple. For larger amounts or regular monthly rotation, basic KYC (PAN/Aadhaar) may be requested to keep everything safe, transparent, and compliant.

Is the process safe and confidential?+

Absolutely. Every transaction is handled with bank-grade security and complete confidentiality. We have two physical offices in Ghaziabad you can visit anytime, and 150+ satisfied clients across Delhi NCR.

Can I transfer to any bank account?+

Yes, you can transfer to any bank account in India. For card-to-card, we can move funds to any credit card as well. Just tell us the destination and we handle the rest.

How do you handle large amounts (₹5L–₹50L+)?+

For high-value transfers, we use structured, properly-paced settlements to keep your bank account in good standing and avoid any fraud flags. Our infrastructure is built to handle large volumes smoothly — we've processed ₹50Cr+ to date.

What are your service charges?+

Our rates start from just 1.75%. Card-to-bank is 2.15% (Visa/MC) or 2.25% (RuPay), and reduces for higher amounts. Check the full pricing table above, or use our calculator to see exactly what you'll receive.
